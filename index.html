How to play this game ?

1- Click ROLL DICE to get 2 to 6 points, these points will be store into your Current Score.

2- Use HOLD to save all your current points into your global score.

3- If you hold your points or get a 1 then it's other player's round to roll the dice.

4- First player to reach 100 points win the game!
