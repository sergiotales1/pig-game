How to play this game ?

1- Click roll dice to get 2 to 6 points, these points will be put into your 'current score'.

2- Use hold to save all your current points into your global score.

3- If you hold your points or get a 1 then its other player's round to roll the dice.

4- First player to get 100 points win the game!
